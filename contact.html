FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I track my order?

Login to your account and visit the "My Orders" section to view real-time tracking updates. You'll also receive tracking information via email and SMS notifications as your order progresses.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment options including eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay, bank transfers, and all major credit/debit cards. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.

How long does delivery take?

Digital products are delivered instantly to your email. Physical items take 2-5 business days within Kathmandu Valley and 3-7 business days for locations outside the valley.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available for eligible products within 7 days of purchase. Please contact our support team with your order details, and we'll process your refund request within 3-5 business days.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes! We use SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your personal and financial information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

How can I contact customer support?

Our support team is available 24/7 via phone at +977 1-2345678, email at support@kunyo.com, or through the contact form above. We typically respond within 1 hour during business hours.
