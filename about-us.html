Our Story

From Passion to Platform

Founded in 2020, KUNYO started with a simple mission: make gaming more accessible for everyone in Nepal. What began as a small gaming store in Kathmandu has grown into Nepal's most trusted platform for game top-ups and digital cards.

Today, we serve thousands of gamers across the country, offering instant top-ups for popular games, gift cards for major platforms, and unmatched customer support.
